My Story

Experienced Technical Project Manager based in the greater Seattle area, leveraging over ten years of software engineering expertise to bridge technical and business needs. Passionate about leading teams, driving innovation, and fostering collaboration to create meaningful impact.

I am skilled at creating original solutions to complex problems, leading technical initiatives, and mentoring teams to achieve shared goals.
